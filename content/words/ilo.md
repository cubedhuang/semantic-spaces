An _ilo_ is something used to accomplish something else. A spoon is an _ilo_ because it's typically used to scoop up food. A phone is _ilo_ because it's used to communicate (among many other things).

:::ex
tok: **ilo** mi li wile e wawa a.
en: I need to charge my **phone**.
:::

The internet and certain platforms on the internet are often referred to with _ilo_.

:::ex
tok: kulupu li lon **ilo Siko**.
en: The community is on **Discord**.
:::

:::ex
tok: mi toki tawa ona **lon ilo** taso.
en: We only talk with each other **online**.
:::
