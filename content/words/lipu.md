The prototypical _lipu_ is a sheet of paper that records some information. As for physical properties, _lipu_ refers to flat, bendable objects, like sheets of paper, leaves, and pancakes.

:::ex
tok: **lipu** mute a li tawa anpa tan kasi.
en: Many **leaves** were falling off the tree.
:::

More abstractly, _lipu_ also refers to documents that record information, like books, blog posts, and academic articles. It's a common headnoun for websites, as in _lipu Kemeka_.

:::ex
tok: suno ale la mi **lipu** e pali mi.
en: Every day, I **document** what I do.
:::
