_ijo_ may refer to anything.

:::ex
tok: mi moku e **ijo**.
en: I ate **stuff**.
:::

_ijo_ universally describes things, ideas, and phenomena. Some speakers will also use it to refer to any kind of action.

:::ex
tok: mi **ijo** wile lon poka ona.
en: I **did the stuff** that I wanted to alongside him.
:::
