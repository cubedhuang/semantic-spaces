_esun_ deals with exchange. This includes commerce. A _tomo esun_ is a place for exchange, like a store. 

:::ex
tok: mi **esun** e moku lon open suno.
en: I **bought** food at the start of the day.
:::

Exchange is not limited to interpersonal trading. When breathing, a person _esun e kon_ around them for oxygen.

Some speakers use **tenpo esun** to refer to weeks, as the cycle of commerce revolves around weekdays and weekends.
