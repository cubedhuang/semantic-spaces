_pali_ refers to work and creation. When _pali_ refers to work, it doesn't necessarily imply a concrete end product. It may also refer to routine work, like a job.

:::ex
tok: tenpo **pali** li pini la mi lukin e sitelen lon lipu Jutu.
en: When **work** (time) ends, I'll watch videos on YouTube.
:::

Many speakers will use use intransitive _pali_ to refer to generic "doing" actions.

:::ex
tok: jan li ken wile **pali** pona li **pali** ike lon tenpo sama.
en: People can want to **do** good and **do** bad at the same time.
:::

Note that this usage does not cover all usages of "do"; describing "doing the dishes" would probably not involve _pali_ except to emphasize the aspect of it being a particular task that requires work.
