_pimeja_ refers to darker colors, like black, dark blue, purple, brown, and gray. Read more about color words in the [guide to color terms](/guide/colors)!

In addition to referring to dark colors, _pimeja_ often also refers to the night.

:::ex
tok: **pimeja** pini la mi lape ike.
en: Last **night**, I slept poorly.
:::

Like other words associated with time, _tenpo pimeja_ may be used if this is unclear.
