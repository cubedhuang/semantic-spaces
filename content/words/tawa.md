_tawa_ describes motion. As a preposition, it describes motion towards a particular destination.

:::ex
tok: mi **tawa** ma Pelin li awen lon suno luka luka.
en: I'mm **going to** Berlin, and I'll be staying for ten days.
:::

As a preposition, _tawa_ may indicate not only a physical destination but also a recipient, beneficiary, or point of view of something.

:::ex
tok: mi alasa e sona sin lon ilo wawa **tawa** pona pi ma mi.
en: I'm researching energy **for** the good of our planet.
:::

:::ex
tok: ona li wile awen e nasin ni, taso ona li ike a **tawa mi**.
en: They want to keep this system, but I really don't like it (lit. **in my view**, it's not good).
:::
