_mun_ refers to celestial objects. The prototypical celestial object is the moon, but _mun_ can refer to all sorts of objects like stars and planets.

:::ex
tok: **mun** suli luka tu tu li tawa sike lon suno mi.
en: Nine **planets** revolve around our sun.
:::

_mun_ also refers to months. This metaphor has [460 realizations](https://datsemshift.ru/shift856) cross-linguistically, making it the most common one in DatSemShift 3.0.

:::ex
tok: suno olin li lon **mun** nanpa tu.
en: The day of love is in the second **month**.
:::

Like with _suno_, speakers may say _tenpo mun_ to emphasize the aspect of time if necessary.
