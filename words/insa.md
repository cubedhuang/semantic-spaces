_insa_ refers to the middle or innards of something. Being _lon insa tomo_ is to be on the inside of a room or house.

:::ex
tok: **insa** pimeja la kalama li pakala e lape mi.
en: A noise disturbed my sleep in the **middle** of the night.
:::

_insa_ may refer to a conceptual middle as well as a physical one.

:::ex
tok: pilin pona en pilin ike la mi **lon insa**.
en: I'm somewhere **between** feeling good and bad.
:::
