_insa_ refers to the middle or innards of something. Being _lon insa tomo_ is to be on the inside of a room or house. If I wake up during the _insa pimeja_, I'm waking up in the middle of the night.

_insa_ may refer to a conceptual middle as well as a physical one; If _pilin pona en pilin ike la mi lon insa_, I'm somewhere between feeling good and bad.
