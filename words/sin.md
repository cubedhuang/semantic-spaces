_sin_ talks about novelty and repetition. Although these may seem like opposites, they both correspond to a new _instance_ of some phenomenon in a pre-existing category of things.

:::ex
tok: mi o pali sin e ni.
en: We need to do this work again.
:::

In this case, there has already been some _pali_, but we need to perform a new instance of that work. _sin_ describes repetition of the task.

:::ex
tok: sona pi tawa ijo la jan Netan li kama e nasin sin.
en: To describe motion, Newton created a new system.
:::

Here, there already exist certain kinds of _nasin_, but Newton creates yet another one. _sin_ describes the novelty of Newton's system.
