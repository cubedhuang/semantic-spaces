_weka_ describes both distance and absence. If something is _weka_, it's far away or removed.

:::ex
tok: tomo ona li **weka** seme?
en: How **far** is their apartment?
:::

:::ex
tok: mi **weka** e nimi ni tan lipu.
en: I'm **removing** this name from the document.
:::

_weka_ may refer to temporal distance in additional to physical distance.

:::ex
tok: tenpo **weka** la kiwen ni li lon ala ma.
en: A long time ago (lit. at a **distant** time), these metals didn't exist on earth.
:::
