_pilin_ refers to senses, both internal and external. When referring to external senses, it typically refers everything that's not _lukin_ or _kute_, including touch, smell, and taste.

:::ex
tok: selo soweli li **suwi tawa pilin**.
en: The cat **feels nice** to pet.
:::

:::ex
tok: mi wile ala **pilin e kon** pi ijo ni a.
en: I don't want to **smell** this!
:::

The internal sense of _pilin_ refers to emotions and opinions.

:::ex
tok: mi lape pona ala la **pilin** mi li kama nasa li kama ike.
en: A lack of sleep really messes with my **emotions**.
:::

:::ex
tok: **pilin mi la** jan o moku ala e soweli.
en: **I believe that** people shouldn't consume animals.
:::
