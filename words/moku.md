_moku_ refers to consumption. This includes both eating and drinking.

:::ex
tok: mi **moku** e kili e telo.
en: I **ate** fruit and **drank** water.
:::

What does consumption mean?

Typically, _moku_ means that the thing being eaten eventually becomes _part_ of the thing eating in some sense. A lunch box doesn't _moku_ the rice in it because we normally don't frame the rice as part of the box itself, but a phone might _moku_ power because it's framed as more integral to the device.
