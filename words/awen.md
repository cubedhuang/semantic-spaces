_awen_ is about fixedness and stability. This can mean physically fixed; if I'm standing or sitting in place, then I'm _awen_. It can also mean fixed in a particular _state_. If I'm currently writing a book, wanting to _awen_ doing so means continuing to write.

_awen_ may refer to protection. Billionaires tend to _awen e_ the system that allowed them to gain their wealth. The walls of a city _awen e_ the city, and people tend to want to _awen e_ their friends.
