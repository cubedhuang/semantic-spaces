_poki_ are containers for things. As physical containers, they typically contain inanimate things. Drawers, cups, and chests are some typical kinds of _poki_.

:::ex
tok: sina wile kama la o **poki** e ijo sina.
en: If you want to enter, put your belongings in a **locker**.
:::

_poki_ also refers to more abstract containers and categories. Roles in Discord servers are often referred to ask _poki_ because they're a kind of category that a user can belong to.

In contrast with _tomo_, a _poki_ is generally something that you put stuff in, and a _tomo_ is generally something that you enter yourself. For instance, a jailer may frame a jail as a _poki_ for people.
