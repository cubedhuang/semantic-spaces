_wile_ is about desires and requirements. With an animate subject, a _wile_ is typically a desire. When _mi wile e moku suwi_, I have a desire for sweets. With inanimate subjects, _wile_ typically becomes a requirement. If _pali ni li wile e tenpo mute_, the project requires a lot of time.

_wile_ only translates well as 'need' when the need and the desire are associated with each other. For instance, I _need_ water, and if I become thirsty, the need for water _becomes_ a desire for water, so _mi wile e telo_. If I need to work for money but dislike my job, it wouldn't be accurate to say _mi wile pali mani_.
