_ala_ refers to nothing. As a modifier, it either negates a predicate or specifies that there are none of something.

:::ex
tok: mi moku **ala** a.
en: I have**n't** eaten!
:::

When used as a modifier on a noun, it normally indicates that there _aren't_ any of that noun involved.

:::ex
tok: jan **ala** li lon tomo ni.
en: There are **no** people in this building.
:::

Some speakers claim to use phrases like _jan ala_ to refer to "non-human things" rather than "no people."
