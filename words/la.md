_la_ is a grammatical "context-marking" particle. Different uses include context marking

- high adverbs "ken la" "lon la" "pona la"
- topicalizing constituents
  - used instead of 'jo', "tomo ni la supa mute li lon"
  - "general preposition replacement"
  - it's often more marked? imo "mi la ni li pona" is more marked than "ni li pona tawa mi" but this could just be me
- subordinate clauses
