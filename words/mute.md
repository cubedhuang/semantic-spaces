_mute_ talks about quantities. If something is _mute_, it is numerous; _jan mute_ refers to "many people."

:::ex
tok: jan pi mute seme li lon?
en: How many people are there?
:::

As a noun, it refers to quantities; _mute jan_ refers to a "quantity of people."

:::ex
tok: mute jan li lili.
en: There aren't a lot of people.
:::
