With _nasin_, Toki Pona draws a conceptual metaphor paths, directions, and methods. Physically, _nasin_ can refer to sidewalks, train tracks, roads, and rivers. If I point anywhere, the direction in which I'm pointing is a _nasin_. Instructions to assembly IKEA furniture are _nasin_. Ways of life like veganism and Islam are _nasin_. Systems like the grammar of a language and socialism are _nasin_.
