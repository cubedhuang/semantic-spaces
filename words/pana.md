_pana_ refers to giving and emission. This can be in a physical sense,

:::ex
tok: mi pana e sike tawa ona.
en: I passed him the ball.
:::

:::ex
tok: kasi ni li pana e kon suwi.
en: This flower smells sweet.
:::

or in a metaphorical sense, like "imparting."

:::ex
tok: mi wile pana e sona tawa jan ante.
en: I want to give knowledge to others.
:::
