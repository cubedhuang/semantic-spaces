_suno_ refers to light and some sources of light. The prototypical _suno_ is the sun.

:::ex
tok: tomo ni la **suno** tu li lon.
en: There are two **lights** in this room.
:::

_suno_ also refers to days. This is one of the most common cross-linguistic metaphors, with 412 realizations in [DatSemShift 3.0](https://datsemshift.ru/shift1007).

:::ex
tok: **suno** tu la mi lon ma Putape.
en: I was in Budapest for two **days**.
:::

Speakers will sometimes say _tenpo suno_ to disambiguate this usage or emphasize the aspect of time.
