_anpa_ refers to lowness, and the _anpa_ of something is the area and stuff below it; to be _lon anpa supa_ is to be below a table, and to be _lon tomo anpa_ is to be in a basement.

Toki Pona draws a conceptual metaphor between physical lowness and lowness in status or ranking. If I win a race, I _anpa e_ the other contestants. In a capitalist society, the workers in a company are _anpa_ to the capitalist.
